# EXL Drives 20% OPEX Reduction for a Leading Title Services Provider

**Type:** Case Study  
**Company:** EXL  
**Client:** Leading Title Services Provider and Insurer

---

## Overview

Discover how a leading title services provider achieved key performance gains through digital transformation and EXL's domain expertise. EXL drove a 20% average OPEX reduction, 40–60% reduction in human errors, and 45% improvement in cycle times.

---

## Challenge

EXL's client, a title services provider, needed to streamline its operations. Their processes involved many complex, interconnected, moving parts — encompassing searching for titles and constant liaising with different agents throughout the process.

In the United States alone, there are over 3,000 counties, which all keep their own property records. These records cover anything that can impact title ownership, such as marriage or death. This EXL client must be sure that the purported owner of a title is in fact the owner by law. This requires an enormous amount of time and effort. The organization had traditionally been wholly reliant on manual processes, including having employees physically visit individual counties to inspect records.

Key challenges included:

- **Scalability issues:** The mortgage market is cyclical by nature, with upturns and downturns. Upturns required the organization to expand its workforce. During downturns, they either had to absorb higher human resource costs or lay employees off.
- **Overreliance on human-centric model:** The organization wanted to move away from this reliance and build a more efficient, fully scalable operating model.
- **Strategic goals:** Better market positioning, explore new revenue streams using data and digital, and pivot towards becoming an innovation leader in the title industry.

---

## Solution

This organization chose EXL as its digital transformation partner for its data analytics capabilities, innovative digital solutions, and deep understanding of the industry. The client also wanted to consolidate into EXL's offshore operating centers to integrate optimal process efficiency for their human-centric model.

### Stage 1: Roadmap Development

EXL worked with the client's C-suite and leveraged agile methodology to assess the client's existing operations across their business. In 12 weeks, EXL provided a roadmap covering:

- Consolidating operations
- Transitioning to transaction-based pricing (TBP)
- Implementing digital and analytics to improve efficiency and customer experience

### Stage 2: Operational Consolidation and Process Excellence

EXL designed and integrated hyperautomation across the client's operational workflow. This included:

- Examining and identifying digital transformation opportunities leveraging AI, robotic process automation, cloud computing, and machine learning to transform business processes
- Scrutinizing proof of concepts and assessing each business process to identify inefficiencies and their solutions

### Stage 3: Conversion to Transaction-Based Pricing

EXL leveraged operational tools and workflow management solutions to measure and benchmark processes. These benchmarks were used to:

- Convert the entire operation into a TBP-led arrangement
- Provide the client with the flexibility and scalability required to cope with the cyclical nature of the mortgage and title industry

### Stage 4: Rapid Digital Transformation

Four EXL digital cloud-native solutions power the client's new technology-led business model:

#### 1. EXL XTRAKTO.AI™
EXL's proven data extraction AI solution extracts critical data elements from unstructured, semi-structured, and structured data such as deeds of trust, closing disclosures (CDs), tax statements, and bank statements to automate and enhance the overall title settlement process.

#### 2. EXL SmartEmail.AI Solution
Intelligent email processing enables the client to massively streamline communication processes. Uses machine learning for:
- Automatic intent recognition
- Natural language entity extraction and response generation
- Integration with cloud-based email servers
- Unified interface to track multiple email inboxes and reply to emails
- Dashboard reporting to provide proactive analytics capabilities to prevent SLA non-compliance by flagging emails that have not been actioned

#### 3. EXL Workflow Automation
Electronically verifies specific data (e.g., whether an individual has paid property tax) in real-time. This real-time view into payment status results in multiple improved business productivity outcomes.

#### 4. SmartAudit.AI Solution
EXL SmartAudit.AI performs audits on **all transactions** (vs. the traditional sampling approach), which provides the client with total visibility in an all-encompassing manner.

## Outcomes

In a relatively short period of time, EXL was able to achieve marked gains for the client.

- **Transition time:** EXL fully consolidated 300+ FTE operations across the title, remittance, and claims services in **3 months** (record timeframe)

| Metric | Result |
|--------|--------|
| Average OPEX reduction | **20%** |
| Reduced human errors | **40–60%** |
| Improvement in cycle times | **45%** (varying between 30% and 70% across title settlement processes) |

**Enhanced customer satisfaction:** The client now enjoys a much more streamlined operational model, which has been optimized for flexibility in response to market changes. It has migrated to a digitally led title service process and generated efficiency and productivity gains across its entire operational workflow.
