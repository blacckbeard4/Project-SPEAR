## Overview

## Challenge

EXL helped the client develop automated trading algorithms for a quant-based emerging market focused hedge fund using customized indices with periodic back-testing trading strategies to evaluate emerging opportunities.

EXL aided the client in achieving:

- Higher annualized returns
- High level of automation
- Data aided decisions in trading
- Consistent performance over benchmark

---

## Data-Led Transformation

EXL leveraged in-house expertise on process automation enabled by AI to expedite trade signal generation to execution. EXL's subject matter experience in trading algorithms helped the client identify trading opportunities quickly and respond to them tactically.

---

## Solution

EXL developed a methodology to provide solutions to the client by focusing on 4 key areas:

1. **Hypothesis Build**
2. **Back-test Strategies**
3. **Algorithm Design**
4. **Performance Tracking**

### Hypothesis Build
EXL identified broad quantitative areas such as market neutral, short-term directional trading, etc. With this overall framework for meta-logic established, trading hypotheses were built.

### Back-test Strategies
- Identified parameters and time frame for back-testing of strategies
- Historical datasets from multiple sources were created for cross-referencing
- Portfolios were built and simulated that allow exposure at ticker level and auto-generation of signals

### Algorithm Design
- EXL formulated detailed meta-logic for all strategies
- With code in **C# & .NET**, automated strategies with direct market access platform were created
- Fully automated black box based trading signals generated by **Bloomberg server API**

### Performance Tracking
- The process was automated to manage the portfolio risk on a real-time basis
- Results are encapsulated in the form of profit and loss with NAV with VAR and cash positions computed daily
