# EXL and Google Cloud Save Leading Global Payments Provider $80M Through Data Modernization

**Type:** Case Study  
**Company:** EXL  
**Partner:** Google Cloud (GCP)  
**Client:** One of the World's Largest Financial Payment Providers

---

## Overview

EXL and Google Cloud partnered to help a leading global payments provider migrate its enterprise data warehouse from a legacy on-premises platform to Google Cloud, resulting in $80M in revenue influence, migration of 1,400+ terabytes of data, and modernization of 1,000+ unique workflows.

---

## Challenge

One of the world's largest financial payment providers faced a highly complex data ecosystem shaped by years of business growth and multiple acquisitions, resulting in substantial amounts of data spread across multiple internal systems. The significant scale and varied structure of this data demanded considerable oversight and resources to manage.

The client turned to EXL, a premier Google (GCP) partner, to lower the substantial licensing costs of these on-premises systems and tools. Achieving this required a forward-looking data strategy aimed at:

- Controlling costs
- Streamlining operations
- Improving workflow efficiencies for sustainable scaling

---

## Solution

EXL focused on migrating the enterprise data warehouse from a legacy on-premises platform to Google Cloud, leveraging its advanced suite of cloud services and technologies.

### Why Google Cloud?

Google Cloud was chosen for its:

- Proven excellence in data-driven decision-making
- Cutting-edge AI and machine learning (ML) tools
- Scalability
- Global infrastructure
- Best-in-class security standards

### Key Innovations

A defining aspect of this initiative was the innovation driven by Google Cloud's technologies and native features, fully integrated into a tailored migration strategy developed by EXL.

Unlike one-size-fits-all approaches, EXL and Google Cloud delivered a fully customized solution. Key innovations included:

- Utilizing Google Cloud's native data migration tools
- AI/ML platforms for advanced analytics
- Seamless integration with the client's existing workflows

This approach:
- Ensured efficient data transfer
- Allowed the client to rapidly embrace new cloud-based capabilities
- Accelerated product launch timelines
- Enabled continuous business innovation

---

## Results

The data migration and modernization delivered measurable, transformative outcomes for the client, ranking among the most significant data migrations in the company's history.

| Metric | Result |
|--------|--------|
| Data migrated | **1,400+ terabytes** seamlessly migrated from on-premises infrastructure to Google Cloud |
| Workflows modernized | **1,000+ unique workflows** systematically transferred and optimized within the new Google Cloud platform |
| Revenue influence | **$80 million** impacted over multiple years |

### Additional Outcomes

- **Cost optimization:** Migrating to a serverless, pay-as-you-go model reduced infrastructure costs and eliminated data center maintenance, enabling long-term savings and financial flexibility
- **Operational efficiency:** Cloud adoption streamlined daily operations, leading to faster product launches and greater workflow efficiency
- **Scalability and performance:** The serverless architecture enabled on-demand scaling, simplified operations by eliminating manual administration and delivered high-speed performance, freeing IT teams to focus on innovation while managing massive data volumes effortlessly

### Future Positioning

With its data infrastructure now modernized and migrated to Google Cloud, the client is well-positioned for scalable, sustainable growth. The new platform:

- Reduces dependency on costly legacy licensing
- Frees up resources for future innovation
- Sets the stage for ongoing solution expansion
- Enables enhanced analytics and AI/ML capabilities
- Supports rapid adaptation to industry changes in the constantly evolving payments sector
