# Campaign Optimizer Framework

**Type:** Case Study  
**Company:** EXL  
**Client:** Asset Management Firm

---

## Overview

EXL helped the client to strategically target and drive their campaigns by streamlining the process and providing clear quantifiable indicators for better transparency and efficiency.

---

## Challenge

Driving higher engagement and identifying effective leads for the campaigns.

Following are the campaigns in which the client aimed to drive higher engagement:

- Digital Campaigns
- Webcast events
- In-person events
- Guest Talks
- Sales Introductions

---

## Data-Led Transformation

EXL leveraged its deep expertise in data assets in the asset management space and utilized machine learning methodologies and analytics frameworks to unlock gains for the clients in terms of return on investment for its marketing dollars.

---

## Solution

EXL developed a methodology to provide solutions to the client by focusing on 4 key areas:

1. **Profile Enrichment**
2. **Leads Generation**
3. **Campaign Design**
4. **Measurement**

### Profile Enrichment
Besides leveraging insights on platform breakdown, advisors, and portfolio from BD data packs, EXL also helped the client in enhancing contact accuracy using past bounces, arbitration heuristics and text-mining sales notes to identify recent product interest and appetite.

### Leads Generation
EXL developed a single, unified opportunity score for each advisor using external sources (Discovery, DST, Broadridge, Factset, Albridge) and BD Data Packs (Morgan Stanley, Wells Fargo, UBS, Edward Jones). Leads were also prioritized based on value, opportunity, discretion as well as recent engagement and behavior.

### Campaign Design
Control selection was a major roadblock for the client. To counter this, EXL devised a methodology to control for changes in segmentation, advisor movement, sales touchpoints, and impact from various other marketing campaigns. Synthetic control groups were developed for advisor and in-person events. KPIs were developed such as lift in flows, momentum, engagement, open and click rates.

### Measurement
In order to measure the effectiveness, EXL developed BI dashboards with integration across data siloes (sales, marketing, and operations) to provide a single platform for tracking real-time performance.

Recommendations on improving an ongoing campaign using an agile test-and-learn framework were also provided.

---

## Results

The solutions provided by EXL resulted in higher engagement rates along with increase in net flows from campaigns.

| Metric | Result |
|--------|--------|
| Net flows from events | **$9B+** |
| Net flows from digital campaigns | **$300M+** |
| Higher engagement rate | **40%** |
| Advisors engaged who had not engaged in the last 12 months | **77%** |
