# Master Data Management

**Type:** Case Study  
**Company:** EXL  
**Client:** Asset Management Firm

---

## Overview

Helping asset managers in acquiring, managing, and enriching the most useful information for accurate business attribution. EXL transformed the existing data pipeline into an intelligent master data framework, delivering long-term value to the clients.

---

## Challenge

The client needed support on their advisor contacts and relationships data management process.

Most of the advisor contacts were from large broker dealers or wire houses having multiple offices and advisors spread across different locations in the US and globally.

The source data came from multiple external publishers that needed to be consolidated through the MDM process and eventually consumed by the business end-users in CRM for tracking and analysis purposes.

Following are few problems the client was facing:

- Data management process
- Support migration process to new CRM system
- Support CRM user requests on any data issue or streamlining requirement

---

## Data-Led Transformation

EXL leveraged multiple 3rd party datasets along with in-house expertise in training and creating rule-based algorithms to transform the existing data pipeline into an intelligent master data framework, delivering long-term value to the clients.

---

## Solution

EXL proposed a data model by implementing a **deterministic-cum-fuzzy matching algorithm** and defined enrichment rules for all key attributes.

Key components of the solution:

- **Hierarchy maintenance:** Leveraged additional attributes, in addition to testing of augmented matching algorithm using machine learning models
- **Data stewardship:** Automated the processes of manual record merge and review of low-confidence unconfirmed records
- **Data governance:** Data validations were performed to ensure that the source, staging and MDM data are all in sync
- **Data Quality dashboards:** Developed to effectively monitor the data issues occurred during ETL load
- **Integration testing:** Various integration tests were performed to validate data flow across systems; inputs to ETL team were routed through JIRA ticketing
- **Validation rules:** Defined to exclude fake entities; developed multiple consolidation rules for different attributes
- **Dual registered advisors:** EXL helped client in retaining multiple organization links for dual registered advisors
- **Attribute level analysis:** Performed to make the most updated publisher win
