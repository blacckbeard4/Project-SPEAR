# End-to-End Automated Reconciliation

**Type:** Case Study  
**Company:** EXL  
**Client:** Top 10 Asset Manager

---

## Overview

Transformation at a top 10 asset manager through end-to-end automated reconciliation. This process transformation resulted in drastic improvement in turnaround time and accuracy, thereby redistributing the efforts of the operations team from processing the data to analyzing the results and other tactical and strategic priorities.

---

## Challenge

For the investment operations team at an asset management firm, a vast majority of the time is spent in reconciling information coming from various physical documents, PDF and spreadsheet-based reports.

With the ever-evolving universe of data coming from various sources (PDF fund prospectus, client request emails, vendor data etc.), there is an increasing need to transform the existing reconciliation process in such business units.

The investment operations team at a leading asset manager was facing the following problems:

- Cumbersome data integration process
- Manual reconciliation and data quality control
- Process workflow management and accessibility of the results

---

## Data-Led Transformation

EXL leveraged strong capabilities in intelligent automation, unsupervised learning and business intelligence to transform the existing human-intensive reconciliation process into an automated self-service solution framework.

---

## Solution

EXL devised a framework for an end-to-end automated reconciliation process for the client. The framework comprised of 4 key components:

1. **Automated data ingestion**
2. **Fuzzy matching & reconciliation**
3. **Business intelligence capabilities**
4. **User interface & feedback mechanism**

### Automated Data Ingestion
EXL developed an ingestion platform that sourced the data coming from various sources (PDF records, fund reports, spreadsheets, vendor data, and internal data warehouse). EXL also devised KPIs for data quality profiling and input validation frameworks with exception alert workflows.

### Fuzzy Matching & Reconciliation
EXL leveraged a sophisticated unsupervised self-learning algorithm to extract data from the input PDFs and triangulate it against internal DB and vendor data. Application Bots were developed that extract and process the matched input files, and further store the reconciliation results in data warehouse.

### Business Intelligence Capabilities
EXL produced dynamic dashboards focusing on reconciliation reports, match rate summary, and exceptions/missing data reports.

### User Interface & Feedback Mechanism
EXL designed the self-service UI, allowing varied user access-levels to manage and export the reconciliation results. The workflow was also enabled to add/edit user permissions, add comments to reconciliation iterations, close or re-open reconciliation cycle.

---

## Results

The solution delivered by EXL has resulted in 90% reduction in turnaround time of the current processes, and 99%+ matching accuracy in the reconciliation process outputs. This has significantly improved the productivity of the investment operations unit at the client.

| Metric | Result |
|--------|--------|
| Reduction in process turnaround time | **90%** |
| Matching accuracy in the reconciliation process/outputs | **99%+** |

This large-scale framework has significantly boosted the productivity of the investment operations team.
